Most apps track what you did today.

I built one that shows how you change over time.

It takes your writing, listening, check-ins, and choices and turns them into a living map of your identity patterns — what's stable, what's shifting, and what brings out different versions of you.

I kept getting asked: "where does my data go?"

No ads. No data brokers. No training AI models on your journal entries. You can delete everything with one tap.
