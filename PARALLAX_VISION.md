# PARALLAX — Product Philosophy

**Tagline:** See yourself from every angle

Parallax is not a tracker. It is a personal pattern recognition engine for the psyche.

## Core Principle

The system synthesizes signals from creative writing, music listening, fitness data, mood self-reports, and behavioral patterns into insights that help users understand:

- Hidden behavioral patterns
- Identity shifts
- Internal conflicts
- Optimal creative states
- Psychological blind spots

The objective is to produce moments where users think: "How did it know that about me?"

## Success Metrics for Insights

The most successful insights will:
1. Connect multiple data sources
2. Reveal patterns the user hadn't consciously noticed
3. Frame those patterns in identity-level language
4. Encourage reflection rather than prescribe behavior

## Design Principles

Parallax should feel like a mirror that reveals meaning in the patterns of a person's life. Insights should be thoughtful, slightly poetic, and reflective rather than purely analytical.

## Five Meta-Archetypes

**Observer** — Understanding reality and patterns
- The Analyst, The Philosopher, The Archivist

**Builder** — Creating structure and tangible progress
- The Architect, The Strategist, The Operator

**Explorer** — Novelty, experience, and creative expression
- The Wanderer, The Creator, The Performer

**Dissenter** — Autonomy and resistance to imposed systems
- The Rebel, The Critic, The Iconoclast

**Seeker** — Meaning, transformation, and self-discovery
- The Pilgrim, The Alchemist, The Visionary

Users don't have one fixed archetype — they move through phases. The system tracks Baseline (historical), Current (today), and Emerging (trending) archetypes.
