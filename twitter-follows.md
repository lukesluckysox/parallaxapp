## How to use this list
- Follow all 20 this week
- Reply to 3-5 of their posts per day with genuine thoughts (not "great post!")
- Quote-tweet anything that connects to what you're building
- Don't DM anyone cold asking them to try your app — let the relationship build first
- After 2 weeks, you'll naturally find more accounts through their networks
